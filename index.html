About Rwandan Culture

Rwanda is rich in tradition and culture, known for its music, dance, crafts, and storytelling. At Inteko y'umuco, we celebrate and preserve the history and vibrant culture of Rwanda. Visitors can learn about traditional customs, wear cultural attire, and experience authentic Rwandan hospitality.

Night Culture Experience

Join us for our special night culture time where you can camp under the stars, share stories, and learn deeply about Rwandan culture through conversations and performances.
